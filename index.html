What is good design?

Good design helps people get what they want from a product with ease. The design shouldn't be frustrating, but be a pleasant experience that fits the audience's needs. This is especially true when it comes to designing products or services people use to accomplish something, like an app or a door. A good design can also effectively convey a message or shape an experience. Colors, fonts and layouts are design elements that when chosen carefully, can evoke certain emotions and desires in the audience.

I haven't gotten to use all of these personally, but I find these products to make everyday tasks much easier and enjoyable.
